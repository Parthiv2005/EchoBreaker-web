Echobreaker Account Deletion Request

This page provides instructions for requesting the deletion of your Echobreaker app account and associated data stored in our systems.

Steps to Request Deletion:

  1. Send an email to: parthivraju2005@gmail.com
  2. Use the subject line: Account Deletion Request - Echobreaker
  3. In the body of the email, please clearly state the email address associated with the Echobreaker account you wish to delete. This helps us verify and locate your account.

Data Deletion Process:

  • Upon receiving your verified request, we will permanently delete the following data associated with your account from our Firestore database:
    • Your email address used for login.
    • Your unique User ID generated by Firebase Authentication.
  • We aim to process deletion requests within 14 business days. You will receive an email confirmation once the deletion is complete.
  • Please note: Anonymized usage data (like crash logs or general app interaction analytics collected via Firebase/Google Analytics), which is not directly linked to your personal User ID or email after deletion, may be retained for analytical purposes to improve the app.

Contact:

If you have any questions about this process, please contact us at parthivraju2005@gmail.com.
